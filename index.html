ABOUT US

Building Trust Through Quality

Bolia Brothers LLP is committed to excellence in agricultural commodity distribution, serving businesses across India with integrity and reliability.

Who We Are

Established on May 1, 2023, Bolia Brothers LLP (LLPIN: ACA-8788) operates in the wholesale trade industry, specializing in the distribution of agricultural commodities and food staples. Based in Udaipur, Rajasthan, we have built a reputation for delivering premium quality products including Basmati Rice, Yellow Corn, Lokwan Wheat, and Wheat & Malt Extract.

With a total contribution of ₹1,00,00,000, we are well-positioned to serve the growing needs of businesses across India. Our commitment to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction sets us apart in the competitive agricultural distribution industry.

Our Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

To provide high-quality agricultural commodities to businesses across India, ensuring reliable supply chains and competitive pricing that support the growth of our partners.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted name in agricultural commodity distribution across India, recognized for our commitment to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction.

Our Values

Integrity, quality, reliability, and customer focus drive everything we do. We build long-term partnerships based on trust and mutual growth.

Our Approach

We leverage our strong network of suppliers and logistics partners to ensure timely delivery of premium products at competitive market rates.
